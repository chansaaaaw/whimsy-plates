Garlic Chicken

Delicious Fried Chicken with Fragrant Garlic!

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (legs, thighs, or breasts)
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (optional)
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with minced garlic, salt, black pepper, soy sauce, and oyster sauce. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Fry the marinated chicken pieces until golden brown and cooked through, about 8-10 minutes per side.
  4. Once cooked, remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice or your favorite dipping sauce. Enjoy!
