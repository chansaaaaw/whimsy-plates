Taiyaki

Delicious Fish-Shaped Cakes Filled with Sweet Goodness!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Sweet filling of your choice (e.g., red bean paste, chocolate, or custard)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together milk, egg, and vegetable oil until well combined.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined (do not overmix).
  4. Preheat a taiyaki pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  5. Pour a small amount of batter into each side of the taiyaki pan, enough to cover the bottom.
  6. Add a spoonful of your chosen filling on top of the batter, then pour more batter on top to cover.
  7. Close the pan and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  8. Remove from the pan and serve warm, dusted with powdered sugar if desired.
