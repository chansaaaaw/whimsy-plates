Authentic Japanese Udon

A Delightful Bowl of Chewy, Handmade Noodles!

Ingredients

  • 300g udon noodles (fresh or dried)
  • 1.5 liters dashi stock (or water)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 200g toppings (e.g., tempura, green onions, kamaboko, or any preferred vegetables)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions

  1. If using dried udon, cook according to package instructions. If using fresh udon, briefly boil until heated through, then drain.
  2. In a pot, combine dashi stock (or water), soy sauce, mirin, and salt. Bring to a simmer.
  3. Add the cooked udon noodles to the pot and gently stir to coat with the broth.
  4. Prepare your desired toppings (e.g., fry tempura, slice green onions, etc.).
  5. Serve the udon in bowls, ladling broth over the noodles and topping with your choice of garnishes.
  6. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top for added flavor.
