Matcha Latte

A Refreshing and Creamy Matcha Latte Perfect for Any Time!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons matcha green tea powder
  • 1 cup hot water (not boiling)
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1-2 tablespoons sweetener (honey, sugar, or maple syrup, to taste)
  • Ice cubes (for iced version)
  • Optional: whipped cream, for topping

Instructions

  1. For the hot matcha latte: In a bowl, sift the matcha powder to remove any lumps.
  2. Pour in the hot water (about 175°F or 80°C) and whisk until frothy and fully dissolved.
  3. In a separate saucepan, heat the milk until warm (or froth it using a milk frother).
  4. Combine the matcha mixture with the warm milk and sweetener, stirring well. Pour into a mug and top with whipped cream if desired.
  5. For the iced matcha latte: Follow steps 1-3 to prepare the matcha and milk mixture.
  6. Fill a glass with ice cubes and pour the matcha-milk mixture over the ice.
  7. Stir to combine and enjoy your refreshing iced matcha latte!
