Halal Dimsum Mentai

Delicious Dimsum with Creamy Mentai Sauce!

Ingredients

  • 300g ground chicken or shrimp
  • 1 cup shrimp, chopped (optional)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger, minced
  • 1 pack dumpling wrappers (halal certified)
  • For the Mentai Sauce:
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp mentaiko (cod roe), optional for halal
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground chicken or shrimp with soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger until well combined.
  2. Place a small spoonful of the filling in the center of each dumpling wrapper. Fold and seal tightly.
  3. Steam the dumplings over boiling water for about 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  4. Meanwhile, mix the mayonnaise, mentaiko (if using), and lemon juice to create the Mentai sauce.
  5. Serve the dimsum hot, drizzled with Mentai sauce and garnished with chopped green onions. Enjoy!
