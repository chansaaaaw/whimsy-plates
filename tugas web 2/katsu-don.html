Curry Katsu Don

Crispy Katsu Chicken Served Over Rice with Flavorful Curry Sauce!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 chicken breasts, pounded to 1/2 inch thickness
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1-2 packs of halal curry roux
  • Chopped green onions, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Set up a breading station: place flour in one bowl, beaten egg in another, and panko breadcrumbs in a third.
  3. Dredge each chicken breast in flour, dip in egg, then coat with panko breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat and fry the chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes per side. Remove and let it rest.
  5. In the same skillet, add sliced onions and saut√© until softened.
  6. Add water or chicken broth to the skillet, and bring to a simmer.
  7. Add the curry roux to the pan, stirring until dissolved and the sauce thickens.
  8. Slice the fried chicken into strips and place it on top of the rice in a bowl. Pour the curry sauce over the chicken.
  9. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious halal curry katsu don!
