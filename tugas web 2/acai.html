Acai Bowl

A Deliciously Nutritious Bowl of Bliss!

Ingredients

  • 2 packets frozen acai puree
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or agave syrup (optional)
  • Chia seeds or coconut flakes (for topping)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the frozen acai puree, banana, and almond milk. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the acai mixture into a bowl.
  3. Top with granola, mixed berries, and your choice of toppings such as honey, chia seeds, or coconut flakes.
  4. Enjoy immediately for a refreshing and energizing treat!
