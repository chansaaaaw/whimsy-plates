Flower Mochi Donut

A Cute and Delicious Treat for Every Occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • ¾ cup milk
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Sprinkles or icing (for decoration)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the eggs, then add milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Make some dough into small round shapes, then put them together.
  6. Fry the donuts in batches until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes on each side.
  7. Drain on paper towels and let cool slightly before decorating.
  8. Decorate with icing or sprinkles as desired. Enjoy your flower shape donuts!
