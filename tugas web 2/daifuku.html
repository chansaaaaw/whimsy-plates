Daifuku Mochi

Soft and Chewy Japanese Rice Cake Filled with Sweet Delights!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mochiko (sweet rice flour)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup water
  • Potato starch (for dusting)
  • Filling options: red bean paste, fresh fruit, or ice cream

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, mix the mochiko, sugar, and water until smooth.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave for 1 minute. Stir, then microwave for another minute, and stir again. Microwave for a final 30 seconds until the mixture is thick and sticky.
  3. Dust a clean surface with potato starch. Transfer the mochi dough onto the surface and let it cool slightly.
  4. Divide the dough into small pieces and flatten each piece into a circle.
  5. Place your desired filling in the center and carefully fold the edges over to seal. Roll into a ball.
  6. Dust with additional potato starch to prevent sticking.
  7. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container for up to a day. Enjoy your delicious Daifuku Mochi!
