Dry Yogurt Bowl

A Refreshing and Healthy Treat Packed with Flavor!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain yogurt (Greek or regular)
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1/4 cup nuts (almonds, walnuts, or your choice)
  • Chia seeds (optional, for topping)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the yogurt with honey or maple syrup until well combined.
  2. Layer the yogurt mixture in a serving bowl or cup.
  3. Add a layer of mixed berries on top of the yogurt.
  4. Sprinkle granola and nuts over the berries.
  5. If desired, add chia seeds on top for an extra crunch.
  6. Enjoy immediately or chill in the fridge for a refreshing treat later!
