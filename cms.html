Crab Mayo Sushi

Delicious Sushi Rolls with a Creamy Crab Filling!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 8 crab sticks, shredded
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • Soy sauce (for serving)
  • Wasabi and pickled ginger (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain and combine with 2 ½ cups water in a rice cooker. Cook according to the rice cooker instructions.
  2. In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved. Once the rice is cooked, transfer it to a large bowl and gently fold in the vinegar mixture. Allow it to cool.
  3. In another bowl, combine shredded crab sticks, mayonnaise, and lemon juice. Mix well to create the crab filling.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down. Wet your hands and spread about ½ cup of sushi rice evenly over the nori, leaving about 1 inch at the top edge.
  5. In the center of the rice, add a line of the crab filling. Roll the sushi tightly from the bottom using the mat, pressing gently as you go. Seal the edge with a little water.
  6. Using a sharp knife, slice the roll into bite-sized pieces. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Serve with soy sauce, and enjoy your homemade Crab Mayo Sushi!
