Hot & Iced Strawberry Latte

A Deliciously Fruity Latte for a Sweet Treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries (or frozen)
  • 2 teaspoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1 cup hot water (not boiling) for hot version
  • 1-2 shots of espresso (or strong brewed coffee)
  • Ice cubes (for iced version)
  • Optional: whipped cream, for topping

Instructions

  1. For the strawberry syrup: In a saucepan, combine the strawberries and sugar. Cook over medium heat until the strawberries break down and release their juices, about 5-7 minutes. Strain to remove seeds and set aside.
  2. For the hot strawberry latte: Prepare the espresso or strong coffee. In a mug, mix the hot water (if using) with the strawberry syrup and espresso.
  3. In a separate saucepan, heat the milk until warm (or froth it using a milk frother).
  4. Combine the milk with the strawberry-espresso mixture, stirring well. Top with whipped cream if desired.
  5. For the iced strawberry latte: Follow steps 1 and 2 to prepare the strawberry syrup and espresso.
  6. Fill a glass with ice cubes and pour the strawberry syrup and espresso over the ice.
  7. Top with cold milk and stir to combine. Enjoy your refreshing iced strawberry latte!
