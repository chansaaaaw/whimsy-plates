About the Creator

Hello, I’m Chansa Nisrin!

Welcome to Whimsy Plates! I’m a student at SMKN 7 Semarang, majoring in Software and Game Development (PPLG), Class X PPLG 3. While I’m busy learning about coding and technology, my other passion is all things sweet and pretty, especially in the kitchen! I created this website to share my love for baking and cooking with others who enjoy trying new, fun recipes.

From fluffy pancakes and cinnamon rolls to appetizing main courses, I believe every dish can be a little piece of art, bringing joy and sweetness into our lives. My goal is to combine my creativity from coding with my passion for food, offering a collection of recipes that not only taste amazing but are also visually delightful!

As someone who loves both technology and the magic of the kitchen, I’m always experimenting, whether it's with code or ingredients. I hope this website inspires you to try new recipes and bring more flavor into your daily life.

So feel free to explore, try out some of my favorite recipes, and join me on this sweet culinary journey!
