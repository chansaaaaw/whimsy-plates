Halal Curry Udon

A Hearty and Flavorful Dish for Comfort Food Lovers!

Ingredients

  • 200g udon noodles
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 potato, cubed
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 300g halal chicken, beef, or tofu, cubed
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1/4 cup curry powder
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped green onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the udon noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onion and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the carrots, potato, and zucchini. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cubed halal chicken, beef, or tofu to the pot and cook until browned.
  5. Pour in the broth and bring to a simmer. Stir in the curry powder and soy sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Add the cooked udon noodles to the pot and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to heat through.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chopped green onions. Enjoy your delicious Halal Curry Udon!
