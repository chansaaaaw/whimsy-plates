OmuRice

A Delightful Japanese Comfort Food!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup halal chicken, beef, or tofu, diced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Ketchup (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until translucent.
  2. Add the diced chicken, beef, or tofu, and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the diced carrot and peas, cooking until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing well. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk. Season with a pinch of salt.
  6. In a non-stick pan, heat the remaining tablespoon of oil over medium heat. Pour in the egg mixture and gently swirl to spread evenly.
  7. When the eggs are mostly cooked but still slightly runny on top, spoon the rice mixture onto one half of the omelet.
  8. Carefully fold the other half of the omelet over the rice. Slide onto a plate.
  9. Drizzle ketchup on top for garnish, and enjoy your delicious Halal Omu Rice!
