Strawberry Bomboloni

Delicious Italian Doughnuts Filled with Cream and Topped with Fresh Strawberries!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • Oil for frying
  • Strawberry jam or pastry cream for filling
  • Fresh strawberries, sliced for topping
  • Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic, about 5-7 minutes. Cover with a cloth and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size, about 1 hour.
  4. Once risen, punch down the dough and roll it into small balls (about 1.5 inches in diameter). Place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Cover the dough balls with a cloth and let them rise again for 30 minutes.
  6. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C). Fry the bomboloni until golden brown on both sides, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  7. Drain on paper towels and let cool slightly. Fill with strawberry jam or pastry cream using a piping bag.
  8. Top with sliced strawberries and dust with powdered sugar before serving. Enjoy!
