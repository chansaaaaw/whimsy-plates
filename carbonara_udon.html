Carbonara Udon

A Creamy and Delicious Twist on Classic Carbonara!

Ingredients

  • 200g udon noodles
  • 100g turkey bacon or sliced smoke beef, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese (ensure it's halal certified)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook udon noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, cook turkey bacon or sliced smoke beef until browned and cooked through. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add the hot udon noodles to the pan with the turkey bacon, then quickly mix in the egg mixture, stirring continuously to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.
